General thoughts on this CA

This was a very hard Course assignment to get motivated for. Having spent five weeks on the project exam and then going straight to another delivery for another 2 weeks was hard. I do see the value of it in both learing to revisit our code and see what changes can be made, but also just for the experience of seeing how far along I have come from the cross course assignment to the project exam.

But there is never enough time. I do feel I made good improvements to all of the projects and I am happy with most of them. Even the cross course project got a little better.

Merry Christmas and a Happy New year!

I look forward to more classes next year.
